INTO THE PERCEPTION is an interactive performance space to feel the body.

Through Kinect's tracking algorithm, body language is read and transferred into interactive sound and graphics, reducing the limitations of graphics and music for dancers and allowing them to be freed from the status of dancer or choreographer.

With real-time interactive graphics, dancers are triggered to think and observe their body movements, as well as the relation between themselves and space, thus giving them continuous powers for improvisation.
