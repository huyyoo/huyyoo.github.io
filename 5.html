The installation Mimics Human Speech.

A magnet is placed in the coil. The Arduino changes the frequency of the electric current passing through the coil. Parameterized controllers regulate the frequency of the diaphragm vibration.

The aluminum pipe changes the volume of air in the cavity and forms words.
